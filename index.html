ABOUT SCHOOL

Welcome to Mrs. Bullmore's Matriculation School! Founded in 1954 by the visionary Mrs. Teresa Bullmore, our school has grown from humble beginnings into a renowned institution that offers high-quality education from Kindergarten through Class X. Located in the serene Mount Pleasant area, we provide an enriching environment for both boys and girls to thrive academically, socially, and culturally.

Read More →

VISION

Mrs. Bullmore's Matriculation School was founded in 1954 by Mrs. Teresa Bullmore an eminent educationist, philanthropist and social worker. The School began in 1954 with 7 students and has now reached a capacity of 1118 offering quality education for both boys and girls.

Read More →

MISSION

At Mrs. Bullmore's Matriculation School, our vision is to create an inclusive educational environment where students from diverse backgrounds can flourish. We are committed to fostering holistic development, emphasizing academic achievement alongside moral, social, and emotional growth.
